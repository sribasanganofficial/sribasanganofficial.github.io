About Us

Sribas Angan Bhakti Vriksha is a spiritually enriching initiative aligned with ISKCON. Our goal is to spread Krishna Consciousness through regular devotional gatherings, scriptural study, and cultural training.

Weekly Satsang

Join us every Monday to Sunday at 4:00 PM or 6:00 PM for our Bhakti Vriksha sessions. These spiritually nourishing gatherings include kirtans, scriptural discussions, japa meditation, and prasadam distribution.

Sessions are available in both online and offline formats. For online classes, join us every Monday, Tuesday, and Thursday at 7:30 PM. The link will be shared upon registration.
